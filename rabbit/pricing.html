Common questions

FAQ

Do you provide channels or content?

No. Rabbit Player is a media player application. It does not include, stream, or distribute any TV channels, movies, or other media. You connect your own IPTV source (M3U playlist, XTREAM Codes, or similar).

How many devices can I use?

Both plans support multiple devices under a single account. Install Rabbit Player on your TV, smartphone, tablet, and desktop — all covered by one subscription.

Can I cancel or switch plans?

Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Switching from 6-month to annual takes effect on your next renewal.

What does "Catchup" require?

Catchup availability depends entirely on whether your IPTV content provider supports it. Rabbit Player surfaces catchup functionality if your source exposes it — we don't add or restrict it.

Where are recordings stored?

Recordings are saved locally on the device where you initiate the recording. Cloud storage for recordings is not included in either plan.

Is there a free trial?

We don't currently offer a free trial. The 6-month plan is a low-commitment way to experience the full feature set before choosing an annual subscription.
